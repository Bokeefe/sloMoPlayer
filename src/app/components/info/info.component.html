this is a slow music player made by brendan okeefe.

it creates 30 track genre playlists, plays the tracks slowly, and adds some reverb. u can further tweak the to find that trippy/square sweet spot.


This is going to be very buggy so expect to wait a really long for a song to load. also, it will explode if you do anything after hitting play. if it gets completely goofy, just re-load the page, pick a genre, arrange the playlist how you want, and play.
